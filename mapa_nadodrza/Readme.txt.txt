Nadodrze is one the Wroclaw`s districts. It`s map contains emotions - stories, photos and movies with memories of this place. Webpage is translated to Polish, English and German languages.

I have made a custom, easy to use, content management system, which communicates with Google Firebase. It allows to add and edit content, photos, descriptions as well as moderate comments.

Tools and technologies used:
Javascript, AngularJS, Google Firebase, Bootstrap, CSS3, HTML5, Photoshop
